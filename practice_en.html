How to Use

Lesson Activities

By clicking on a picture, you'll find several different activities. Do whichever ones are fun for you. Or, if it feels too much like studying and not enough like fun, you can just watch the movie and enjoy it!

Using Subtitles

It's tempting to watch the movie with subtitles in your own language. But, for many people, this makes it difficult to listen. We recommend that you avoid this and use subtitles in English. Just be aware that some of the subtitles have mistakes that we can't control.

Online Lessons

You can join us for online speaking practice in a group. Here, you can practice common sentences in English while using themes and vocabulary from the movies. Contact us if you want to find out more, or look at one of our sample lessons here.
